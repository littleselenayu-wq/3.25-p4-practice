Fun Facts about Sombr

1. He went to school with famous alumni: attended the prestigious LaGuardia High School for music, the same performing arts school as Timothée Chalamet and Nicki Minaj.


2. "Caroline" changed everything: At age 17, his single "caroline" went viral in 2022, leading him to drop out of high school to pursue music full-time [1, i-d.co].


3. He's a "chronically online" creator: While known for his moody music, he has a secret TikTok account (@sombrsucks) where he posts jokes and sketches aimed at Gen Z, according to NBC's coverage of the artist.


4. Self-taught producer: He started crafting beats on GarageBand in middle school before advancing to Logic Pro X, often producing his own material, notes this YouTube video [4, Wikipedia].


5. He's a VMA winner: Shortly after releasing his debut album, I Barely Know Her, in 2025, he won a Moonman trophy at the MTV Video Music Awards for the "Back to Friends" video, as reported by the BBC.


6. NYC roots meet LA sounds: Although he is based in Los Angeles, he was born and raised on the Lower East Side of New York City, which influences his indie-rock sound [8, Wikipedia].


7. He writes all his own songs: In a modern pop landscape full of co-writers, Sombr is known for writing his own music, a detail highlighted by The Hollywood Reporter.


8. He was bullied for his music: Before the fame, he was bullied for making music in high school, but he continued to post his songs online anyway, as discussed in this BuzzFeed article.


9. He wants a smoothie deal: Sombr has joked that he wants a brand deal with Erewhon, the upscale LA market, according to an interview with i-D
